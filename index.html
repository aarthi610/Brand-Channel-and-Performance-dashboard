Analyst Insights & Action Points
🚀
App is your Growth Engine
The App channel drives 42.8% of revenue (₹78K) with only a 5–10% avg. discount — far more margin-friendly than Amazon (15–22%). Double down on app-exclusive deals and push notifications to protect margin while scaling volume.
⚠️
Amazon Eats Margin
Amazon generates the lowest revenue (₹14K, 7.6%) while carrying 15–22% discounts across SKUs. Consider rationalising the Amazon catalogue to only high-velocity products like Vitamin C Serum and Collagen Supplement.
📍
Jaipur & Hyderabad Are Untapped
These two cities account for 43% of total revenue yet likely have room for localised campaigns. Consider region-specific influencer partnerships and WhatsApp retargeting in these metros to capture repeat purchases.
💎
Wellness Rivals Skincare
Wellness (₹65.3K) and Skincare (₹64.7K) are almost equal contributors — an unusual mix for a beauty brand. Collagen Supplement alone brings ₹20.4K. Cross-sell Wellness bundles to Skincare buyers for LTV uplift.
🎯
Vitamin C Serum is the Anchor SKU
With ₹30.7K revenue (16.8% of total), 18 units sold across all channels, and hero status, this SKU drives discovery. Bundle it with Night Cream or Face Wash to raise basket size — both are natural skincare pairings.
📅
May & March Are Peak Months
May (₹42.4K) and March (₹25.1K) saw the highest volumes — likely driven by summer skincare prep and early-year buying. September was the lowest (₹1.3K). Plan inventory and ad spend spikes around these months in 2025.
